NASA

My vision is a future for humanity where we will be completely free to pursue activities outside of our planet. - Franklin Chang Diaz

NASA is the space agency for the USA. They are one of the leading space agencies in the world and are responsible for the civilian space program as well as the research of space so we can further understand more about our planet and our place in the universe. Over the years NASA has had numerous achievements including the first space agency to reach the moon, joint creation of the International Space Station and Voyager 1 and 2 which are exploring space outside of our solar system.

Currently, they are working on new programmes such as the Artemis Program in which NASA is aiming to send more people to the moon by 2024 using a range of new technologies so that they can explore more of the lunar surface than has ever been explored before. They are collaborating with commercial and international partners such as Blue Origin. The Space Launch System is another one of NASA's current aims to develop a heavy-lift launch vehicle so that we can expand our presence to destinations beyond Earth.

NASA are also interested in Mars and this year they will be sending the Perseverance Rover to the martian surface so that further knowledge on Mars can be collected. This new rover aims to address key questions about whether there is life on Mars and also to explore ahead before the mission to Mars occur so that the challenges faced can be addressed.
