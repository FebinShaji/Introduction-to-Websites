Spacex Nasa Blue Origin

SpaceX:

SpaceX is an American space company whose CEO is Elon Musk. SpaceX has numerous goals which they want to achieve in the coming years. These include reducing the cost fo space flight thus making it available for commercial purposes, developing rocket technology to allow us to reach Mars and eventually also touse space flight as a means of travelling around the world rather than flights.

Blue Origin:

Blue Origin also has similar goals to other space companies. One of their main aims is to also reduce the cost of space travel so it can be made widely available to the public. They are also working on travelling to the moon and setting up bases and exploring the lunar surface.

NASA:

NASA is the US space agency and over the past years they have been cooperating a lot with commercial companies such as SpaceX and Blue Origin. They are interested in revisiting the Moon however this time they want to carry our further studies about the lunar surface and gain a deeper understanding of it. They are working with multiple space companies to achieve this goal.
