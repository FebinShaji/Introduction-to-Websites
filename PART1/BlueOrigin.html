Blue Origin

Blue Origin aims to preserve the Earth and make use of other planets, moons and asteroids to mine for resources that can be transported back to the Earth. They also have plans to move industries and factories to other planets, or moons so we can reduce the amount of pollution on our planet, thus meaning that we can keep our home planet protected.

Blue Origin to achieve their goal of utilizing other planets for the benefit of Earth are working on creating reusable rockets that will lower the cost of travelling in space meaning that it is a viable solution. They are working on being able to reuse the rockets first stage with minimal refurbishment, lowering the cost of launch and increasing the rate at which these rockets can be launched. They are working to develop rockets that after launch can return to earth and land safely which will again decrease the cost of launch and increases the availability for customers. They are developing rockets that can carry as great a payload as possible whilst still trying to lower the cost thus benefitting their customers even more.
